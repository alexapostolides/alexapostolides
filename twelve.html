pizza character studies

This is a character development and 3D animation exercise. I designed and illustrated three separate characters that all have distinct personalities and traits, which are reflected in their posture, orientation, and action. I then created 3D models of each character in Maya, rigged a FK and IK skeleton to the model, and animated each one. While animating, I focused on subtle motions that best conveyed their personalities.

"Hello"

It was a lot of fun creating the personalities for these characters. On the left is Ronnie, a pepperoni slice who is too cool to acknowledge your presence. The big guy in the middle is Chucky, an all cheese personal pie, who also has a big heart. Last but not least is Hamlet, who is a timid ham and pineapple slice.
