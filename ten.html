my ravaged head of hair

A poster and video spot for a contemporary opera based upon a poem written by Eduardo Galeano. My Ravaged Head of Hair is about a man who is getting old but retains his confidence and creativity. My goal was to convey a sense of playfulness and professionalism within the poster.
