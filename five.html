bauhaus animated poster

An animated augmented reality concept for a poster using Cinema 4D. When looking for posters to play off of, I saw potential in Herbert Bayer’s Bauhaus Ausstellung, 50 Jahre poster because it represents the school’s philosophy of how everything breaks down into simple geometric shapes and colors. Using this as inspiration, I constructed & deconstructed famous Bauhaus objects into one another.
